How do I read my meter?

Reading your meter is easy, once you know what the numbers on it mean. Your meter has one row of numbers - for most meters this is eight.

The five numbers (black) on the left-hand side represent kilolitres (1000 litres, and these are the numbers you will be recording on this site, which are the readings the municapality also records for your monthly bill.

The three numbers (red) on the right represent litres,
you will not be using these red numbers.

In the diagram above, the meter represents 126 kilolitres and 487 litres.
